Why Race?

Support Cougar Athletics

Free T-Shirt

All participants will receive a special event t-shirt, and additional treats will await the young racers at the finish of the Kids Fun Run.

Historic Village Route

The 5K run and walk route takes participants through the historic coastal village of Castine with a brief trail run in Witherle Woods.

The Route

Starting at the bottom of the Town Common, the race takes a quick turn up hill on Main Street towards Battle Avenue. A jog to the left and one last short climb on Wadsworth Cove road takes runners behind Fort George and into Witherle Woods. A short distance down a wooded trail and over the highest point in Castine and then we're back on roads headed way downhill. Emerging from Witherle Woods onto Battle runners zig and zag down Madocawondo meeting up Perkins Street. A beautiful run along the waterfront leads to the last big downhill section into downtown Castine. Don't stop to grab a bite at T&C Grocery, however, as there's only about a mile to go, circling back on Water Street and then up to Court Street, ending where we began.
